# Movement Timer Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-03

Movement Timer does not collect, sell, or share your personal data.

## Data stored on your device

Timer session history and app settings are stored locally on your device using on-device storage (SQLite and local preferences). This data is not transmitted to our servers.

## No account required

The app does not require sign-in.

## Notifications

If you allow notifications, the app uses them only to alert you about timer events. Notification permission can be revoked in iOS Settings.

## Audio

The app plays short sounds for timer feedback. It does not record audio.

## Third parties

We do not use analytics, advertising, or third-party SDKs that collect personal data.

## Children

The app is not directed at children under 13.

## Changes

We may update this policy; the “last updated” date will change.
