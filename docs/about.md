# Movement Timer

I kept reaching for different timer apps depending on what I was doing—a workout, a focus block, something on the stove—so I built one that covers the basics in a single place.

Movement Timer gives you interval, stopwatch, and pomodoro timers with a simple, native-feeling setup screen. Pick a category, set your durations, add an optional note, and go. When you're done, sessions land in History so you can look back or clean up old runs.

No account. No feed. No upsell funnel. Your data stays on your device.

If something feels off or you want a small tweak, I'd genuinely like to hear from you.
